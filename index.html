All-in-One Sidebar

Four Powerful Tools, One Clean Interface

Generator, Downloader, Video converter and Settings — all accessible from a sleek accordion menu. Switch between tools instantly without leaving your Flow AI workspace. Pro badge, usage stats and quick guide included.

Prompt Generator

Batch Generate Hundreds of Images

Paste all your prompts at once, set a wait time, and hit start. The extension feeds each prompt to Flow AI one by one, automatically. Attach a character image for consistent results across all generations. Supports 30 languages.

Smart Downloader

Scan, Match & Download Instantly

Enter your target prompt numbers and start scanning. The bot automatically scrolls through all your images, reads prompt numbers, and collects matching ones. Choose between Image and Video mode, adjust speed, and organize downloads into folders.

Image to Video

Convert Your Images to Videos

Scan your generated images, write a video prompt for each one, and let the bot handle the rest. It selects each image from the gallery, enters your prompt, and starts video generation — all automatically, one by one.
